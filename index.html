About Ellen

Ellen Finlay is a London-based Contemporary Dance Artist. Originally from Dublin and recently graduated from Trinity Laban, Ellen has a wide range of experience in the realms of teaching, choreography and performance. She has worked with choreographers such as Gary Lambert, Jessica Wright and Marina Collard whilst training, as well as performing and presenting her own work across London in venues such as Southwark Platform and the Artworks Elephant.

Ellen is interested in politically motivated work, work that provokes conversation and engages audiences. She is currently interested in widening her practice and connecting with like-minded London-based Artists.

Teaching

Ellen is an experienced and qualified teacher and holds a diploma in Dance in Dance Instruction (ISTD). She has experience teaching Ballet, Jazz and Contemporary Dance, and is a confident choreographer. It is possible to book Ellen for Workshops for all ages and levels, see "Contact" for more information.
